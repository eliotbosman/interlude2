Interlude

Inter
lude

From a break in time during a performance or event, to the empty space between two forms. How can we take advantage of a break in different ways? How can we shape a physical break? Through contrast of materials and colour, we will reinterpret the meaning of interlude by creating a calm and inviting environment, where we can highlight our furniture pieces and interplay of different forms, creating a natural rhythm in the space.

About


Every year at Beckmans, the final year Product Design students work on a six month collaboration with some of Sweden's leading furniture companies. The final prototypes are presented at the Stockholm Furniture Fair 2023. Participating companies this year are G.A.D, Gärsnäs, Johanson Design, Lammhults, NC Nordic Care and Storängen.
